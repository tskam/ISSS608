Take-home Exercise 4: Visualising and Analysing Daily Routines

Putting Visual Analytics into Practical Use

The Task

With reference to bullet point 3 of Challenge 2 of VAST Challenge 2022, you are required to reveal the daily routines of two selected participant of the city of Engagement, Ohio USA. For the purpose of this take-home exercise, you are required to use ViSIElse and other appropriate visual analytics methods.

Submission Instructions

This is an individual assignment. You are required to work on the take-home exercises and prepare submission individually.

  • The take-home exercise submission must be written by using distill for R Markdown. It can be in either distill article or distill blog format. You are required to publish the exercise on Netlify and provide the link on the take-home exercise submission page on elearn.
  • You are also required to push the take-home exercise folder and data onto your individual Github repository and provide the link on the take-home exercise submission page on eLearn.

Submission date

Your completed take-home exercise is due on 22nd May 2022, by 11:59pm evening.
